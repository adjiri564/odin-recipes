Jollof


Descrption

Jollof rice is a porpular dish in Ghana.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Peacook Thai Jasmine Rice
  • 5 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 fresh pepper
  • 2 onions
  • 4 egg
  • Salt
  • 5 spoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon of curry
  • 1/4 teaspoon of kitchen glory spice

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs allow to cool remove shell and slice into two
  2. Pour the fresh tomatoes into a pot and cook ath high heeat till almost all the water has dried
  3. Add vegetable oil, sliced onions and the tomato puree Stir very well and fry at very low heat and stir at short intervals till the oil has completely seperated from the tomato puree
  4. Pour chicken stock and the tomato stew into a cooking pot and leave to boil
  5. Add the drained peacock rice, coriander, seasoning cubes, garlic, salt and pepper to taste. Stir and combine well, cover the pot and leave tp cook on low to medium heat. When the water is almost dried up, then throw in the vegies, and cook for about 4 minutes
  6. To ensure that the peacock rice doesnot burn before the water dries up, the water level should be the same level as the peacook rice. That way, all the water dries up by the time the peacock is cooked.
